# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our standards

**Expected behaviour:**
- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Give and accept feedback gracefully
- Focus on what's best for the project and community

**Unacceptable behaviour:**
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks of any kind
- Trolling or deliberately inflammatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without consent

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported by opening an issue or contacting the maintainer directly via GitHub. All reports will be reviewed and handled appropriately.
